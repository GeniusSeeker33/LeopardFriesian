Breed Standard Charter

Defining what it means to be a true LeopardFriesian.

Conformation Standards

  • Proportional body symmetry and uphill balance.
  • Well‑angled shoulder and strong, straight legs.
  • Balanced neck and refined head structure.
  • Powerful hindquarters supporting sport‑baroque movement.
  • Adequate bone density and sound joints.

Temperament & Mind

Registered horses demonstrate willingness, trainability, and emotional stability. Aggressive or unstable dispositions are disqualifying.

Genetic Requirements

  • DNA and microchip verification required for entry.
  • Friesian percentage minimum per tier (F1/F2/F3+).
  • Confirmed LP genotype and approved pattern source.
  • Health testing (HYPP N/N and similar markers).
