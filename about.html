About the LeopardFriesian Registry

Building a permanent digital institution connecting equine heritage and modern ledger technology.

Our Purpose

The LeopardFriesian Registry preserves and verifies every Friesian Appaloosa lineage record on the Hedera Hashgraph network — an immutable system for registration, transfer, and pedigree validation.

We combine traditional horsemanship values with next‑generation transparency so breeders and owners can trust their registry forever.

Technology & Philosophy

  • Immutable Lineage Records — Each registration & transfer anchored on‑chain.
  • Real‑time Ownership Transfers — Trustless, paper‑free history.
  • Seamless Migration — Excel / Access / SQL imports become ledger entries.
  • Transparency & Security — Fraud‑proof microchip & genetic data.

Phoenix: Foundation Sire

FDAS Phoenix — Registry Token 0001 — is the living origin of this registry. Every approved foal links back to his blockchain lineage, ensuring permanent ancestry verification.
