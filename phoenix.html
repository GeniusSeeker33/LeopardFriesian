His Story

Phoenix isn’t just a horse — he’s a legacy vessel. As the final foal of Brody × Mandy, he carries the early years, the transformation, the bond, and the training journey that define the Leopard Friesian story.

His journey continues through the Attuned Horsemanship Series, the Frozen Gold vault, and the “Echoes of the Bloodline” vision for the future of the program.

What Phoenix Represents

  • A living story of trust, attunement, and transformation
  • The foundation for Frozen Gold breeding packages and future foal legacy plans
