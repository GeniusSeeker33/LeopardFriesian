Champion & Crowd Favorite

Confetti—known to many as Patton—has become one of the most recognizable Leopard Friesian success stories: striking spots, massive presence, and the kind of temperament people remember after they meet him.

His owner had him gelded, and also froze some semen so his legacy can still live on through future breeding.
