Where It Began

The Leopard Friesian journey began with one clear vision: create a horse with presence, intelligence, movement, and a look you don’t forget. A horse that feels like a myth — but lives and breathes in the real world.

That vision became a breeding story anchored by two foundation horses: a Friesian stallion named Brody and an Appaloosa mare named Mandy. Together, they produced a line of foals with unmistakable character and striking pattern — the beginning of a bloodline that would shape everything that followed.

“This is not just breeding. It’s legacy work — creating the kind of horse people feel in their bones.”

FDAS Phoenix — The Final Foal

FDAS Phoenix (born 7/25/2013) is the final foal from Brody and Mandy — the culmination of everything you learned, refined, and protected along the way.

Phoenix is the horse that carries the future of the bloodline. Not only in his genetics, but in his presence: the way he moves, the way he thinks, and the way people feel around him.

What Phoenix Represents

  • Legacy: the closing of one chapter and the opening of the next
  • Consistency: proven bloodline with repeatable quality
  • Intentionality: selective breeding to protect outcomes
  • Vision: building a recognized Leopard Friesian line with real-world value

Frozen Gold

“Frozen Gold” is more than a name — it’s the commitment to preserve Phoenix’s genetic legacy the right way. Frozen semen allows this bloodline to move forward with intention: with planning, veterinary excellence, and the right mares.

This program is selective by design. The goal is not to create the most foals — it’s to create the best foals, with the best match, and the best chance of success.
