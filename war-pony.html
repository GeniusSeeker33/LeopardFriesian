About this launch

War Pony Genesis is the first public chapter of FDAS Phoenix’s story on Hedera. This limited collection of 30 NFTs introduces Phoenix to collectors and supporters with restraint and clarity — a legacy release, not a hype event. This Genesis drop also marks the beginning of the physical infrastructure that will support Phoenix’s long-term legacy.

Where the Launch Proceeds Go

War Pony Genesis is not only a digital release. It supports the real-world infrastructure required to preserve and grow the FDAS Phoenix bloodline.

Proceeds from this collection are directed toward foundational farm improvements that allow Phoenix’s legacy to continue for decades.

Current priorities include:

  • Installation of permanent perimeter fencing and safe pasture systems
  • Construction of a new barn and run-in shelters for mares and foals
  • Farm equipment for producing and harvesting our own hay
  • Land stewardship, pasture development, and regenerative agriculture

These are long-term investments designed to create a sustainable home for Phoenix’s lineage and the future horses who will carry it forward.

Collectors are not only acquiring a piece of digital history — they are helping build the physical environment that makes the bloodline possible.

Meet FDAS Phoenix

FDAS Phoenix is a Friesian Appaloosa stallion representing nearly two decades of intentional, purpose-driven breeding through Friesian Dreams and Spots (FDAS). Phoenix is a real, living horse, stewarded with long-term care and professional handling.
