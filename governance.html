Governance & Breed Council

Ensuring high‑integrity oversight and transparent development of the LeopardFriesian Registry.

Council Composition

  • Desiree Thayer — Founder / Registry Architect
  • Two Respected Breeders — appointed peers representing program diversity
  • Veterinary Advisor — health and genetics standards oversight

Governance Principles

  1. Scientific accuracy in record‑keeping.
  2. Ethical breeding and owner conduct.
  3. Transparency and open audit trail on Hedera.
  4. Long‑term continuity beyond individual ownership.

Implementation Roadmap

  1. Write the Breed Standard Charter.
  2. Define Registration Tiers.
  3. Establish Governance Structure.
  4. Mint Phoenix Foundation Registry NFT (ID 0001).
  5. Build the Online Registration Portal.
  6. Onboard the First 3–5 Approved Horses to Chain.

Future Participation

Breeders and owners interested in joining the Council or registry development can submit an endorsement request once Phase 5 is live.
